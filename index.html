About This Research

Study Area
Ubungo Municipality, Dar es Salaam — 14 wards spanning urban core to peri-urban fringe
Study Period
2022–2025 · 488 geo-referenced residential land price observations
Model
GTWR · Global R² = 0.891 · Adjusted R² = 0.890 · AIC = −1,166.7
Key Predictors
Night-time light (r = +0.83) · Population density · BRT, road & CBD accessibility
Key Finding
Median price +71% (775k → 1.325M TZS). Core–periphery ratio: 14.7× (Sinza vs Kibamba)
Projection
2040 scenario at 19.6%/yr CAGR — WNW & NE corridors reach highest value tier
Data Source
Ministry of Lands Indicative Land Values 2022 · OSM distance layers · NASA nighttime light
Dashboard Contains
Spatial maps · price trends · model diagnostics · correlations · 2040 projection
